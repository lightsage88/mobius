MARVELous Bookworm lets you discover comic book crossover events involving Marvel characters that you search for. Each listing becomes a link you can click on to purchase the story!

But wait...what's a crossover event?

A crossover in comic book terms only occurs when a story spans more than one title. "Crossover events", are collections of titles in which major occurrences are shown as affecting most or all of the stories in the shared universe; in this case the Marvel Comics one.

Examples:
  • Marvel Civil War
  • The Infinity War
  • Avengers vs. X-Men
